A Letter From Us

You Have a Choice.

Momma's Boys is a strategic business advisory providing deep-rooted analytical insight, scrupulous long-term economic planning, and unprecedented storytelling — to help you and your organization reach unfathomable heights, all while promising protection and posterity for Mother Earth.

"Is it possible to navigate the entrepreneurial world without leading with a pecuniary perspective? There is. And it is imperative that we do so."

— The Industrial Involution Manifesto

Not just for the sake of our own lifetime — but for all the generations that succeed us. It means extending our business' time horizons far into the future. Forgoing the greenwashing, the long-washing, our addiction to capital gains, ROIs, GDP, and shareholder values blinded by the myopic view of short-termism.

It is time to engender a new economy with a radically different guiding purpose — one that honors both the needs of humans and the biophysical constraints of the planet. To place the pillars of deep-time humility, legacy mindset, intergenerational justice, cathedral thinking, holistic forecasting, and transcendent goal into the foundation of our commercial constitutions.

We are not just offering consulting expertise. We are asking you to join a cultural revolution. The Industrial Involution is here, right now. You have the choice to be the Lorax or the Once-ler. Let us help you be a guardian of the future, a protector of the planet, a successful entrepreneur — and most importantly, a good ancestor.

We look forward to working with you.
